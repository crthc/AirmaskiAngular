THE INVISIBLE PROBLEM

The most dangerous particles in the air are impossible to see with the naked eye. That’s why a lot of people don’t know about the health risks associated with bad air, because it's invisible. You wouldn’t eat rotten food or drink dirty water because you can see that it’s bad for you, so why settle for dirty air?

Are you still doubting about wearing a mask?

A recent demonstration by the Microbiology Lab at Providence Sacred Heart Medical Center & Children's Hospital shows how wearing a mask can protect you (and others) in close proximity and even at a safe 6ft distance.

Viruses like COVID-19 can be transmitted when droplets are produced through common behaviors, such as coughing, talking, singing or sneezing. Masks are effective at blocking most of these droplets, even when up close.

HEALTH BENEFITS

The quality of the air we breathe affects our bodies in numerous ways. Cleaning the air can provide you with the following important health benefits:

  • Decreased allergies and asthma symptoms
  • Improved health & longer life span
  • Cleaner lungs
  • Improved digestion
  • Improved mental health & mood
